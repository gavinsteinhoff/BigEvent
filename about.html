About The Big Event

Making a difference in Topeka and nationwide. The BIG Event was established in 2012 and has Service, Growth, Inclusion, and Connection at its core. We are students, friends, and your neighbors coming together to make a difference in the lives of our friends and neighbors.

Service

It makes sense that we have service as one of the core values of this organization, at the end of the day this is what we want all of our participants to walk away with and to remember.

We want all students from any background to feel as though they can make a difference in the lives of those around them, this is why we include as many people as we can to help.

Inclusion

Growth

We love to give to our community, but we want to give back in a way that will help you come back year after year. We initiate change in the heart of our volunteers as they grow as leaders, friends, and neighbors.

You leave things better than you find them when you feel connected to the organizations you will help. The most change happens when you do things from the heart.
